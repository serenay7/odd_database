Welcome to ODTÜ ODD Database!

Here you can manage the ODD Database anytime from anywhere quickly.

  • Add: To add a new entry, click the add button next to the name of the table.
  • Edit: There are 4 functions you can do under the editable table.
    • Search: You can search for a text in cells using the search bar on the top-left of the page.
    • Sort: You can sort each column (ascending and descending order) by clicking on the name of the column.
    • Edit: You can edit values of cells by clicking on the cells.
    • Delete: You can delete values of the cells by clicking on cells and deleting values. You can delete an entry by deleting the value x in the "Delete" column of that row.
  • Monthly Payment Orders: You can create payment orders for the months you specify under the monthly payment orders tab.
  • Reports: You can see the types of reports you can create under the Reports tab and create the report you want by clicking on its name.
